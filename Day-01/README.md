# Day 1 – What is SCADA?

## Objective

Understand what SCADA is and why it is used in industry.

## What I Learned

### What does SCADA stand for?

SCADA stands for **Supervisory Control and Data Acquisition**.

It is a system used to monitor and control industrial processes from a central location.

---

## Where is SCADA used?

- Power Generation
- Oil & Gas
- Water Treatment
- Manufacturing
- Smart Buildings
- Transportation

---

## Main Components

- PLC (Programmable Logic Controller)
- RTU (Remote Terminal Unit)
- HMI (Human Machine Interface)
- SCADA Server
- Communication Network

---

## Key Takeaway

SCADA enables operators to monitor equipment, collect data, receive alarms, and control industrial processes efficiently.
